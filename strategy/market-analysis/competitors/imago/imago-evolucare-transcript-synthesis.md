- The product is repeatedly described as integrated, parameterizable, and tied to workflow continuity rather than standalone capture.

## Recurrent buyer pain

- Organizations want less duplicate entry and less manual mapping between systems.
- Managers want faster access to reliable operational and financial signal.
- Field teams want mobile capture, offline use, and role-based access close to the act of work.
- Sector-specific workflows still matter: AHI, PDE, PH, medico-social, RH, and planning each appear as distinct surfaces.

## Why IMAGO may already satisfy the alert-to-workflow step

The transcript set suggests that IMAGO is already built to do a lot of the work that a generic alerting layer would otherwise try to claim:

- it can capture a situation inside a workflow rather than leaving it as free text
- it can distribute that situation to the right role, site, or module
- it can keep the dossier, planning, transmissions, and reporting tied together
- it can turn a field event into a process step, a document, or a traceable operational item

That is why the most plausible DMG opening is not “create an alert and form because IMAGO cannot do it.”

The narrower opening is: take a high-friction situation that already exists in IMAGO and add a governed decision layer on top of it when several roles need to weigh in, compare options, or formalize a recommendation.

## Recurrent claims worth retaining

- Mobile access is framed as an extension of the existing stack, not a separate product story.
- Accessibility and user-portal work show that the stack keeps expanding around the core DUI.
- The AI speaker explicitly frames the tool as a synthesis and drafting assistant and says it is not a replacement for professional judgment.
- RH and planning are positioned as part of the same operational system as the core case file.

## Implication for DMG

- The corpus strengthens the view that IMAGO already covers a mature baseline.
- DMG should stay framed as a non-invasive layer only if it adds a clear decision or governance benefit.
- The strongest opening remains a narrow layer that improves signal, not a replacement stack.
- If IMAGO already handles the alert or the record, DMG should be framed as the layer that collects the relevant views, applies agreed weighting, and returns a compact recommendation or decision trace.

## How to introduce DMG to IMAGO users

Use language that starts from their current stack and avoids sounding like a replacement:

- DMG is a decision layer that sits beside IMAGO when a situation needs structured consultation, weighting, or governance before the next action is fixed.
- DMG does not try to replace the DUI or re-run the dossier; it takes a specific recurring problem and turns it into a clearer, more deliberate decision process.
- If IMAGO already handles the alert or the record, DMG only needs to consume that trigger, collect the relevant views, and return a compact recommendation or decision trace.
- The pitch should be framed as prudence and decision quality, not as a second operational system.

Short introduction line:

- DMG helps when IMAGO already has the event, but the organization still needs a better way to arbitrate what the event means and what should happen next.
