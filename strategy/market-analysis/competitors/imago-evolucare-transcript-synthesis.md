# IMAGO Transcript Synthesis

## Source corpus

This note summarizes the imported transcript corpus under [IMAGO YouTube Transcript Corpus](imago-evolucare-transcripts/README.md).

## Recurrent product language

- IMAGO is presented as a broad medico-social stack, not a single-form tool.
- The recurring surfaces are dossier, planning, transmissions, RH, payroll, accounting, analytics, and mobile access.
- The product is repeatedly described as integrated, parameterizable, and tied to workflow continuity rather than standalone capture.
- Several videos emphasize interfaçage, reduced ressaisie, and better pilotage as the value of the stack.

## Recurrent buyer pain

- Organizations want less duplicate entry and less manual mapping between systems.
- Managers want faster access to reliable operational and financial signal.
- Field teams want mobile capture, offline use, and role-based access close to the act of work.
- Sector-specific workflows still matter: AHI, PDE, PH, medico-social, RH, and planning each appear as distinct surfaces.

## Recurrent claims worth retaining

- Mobile access is framed as an extension of the existing stack, not a separate product story.
- Accessibility and portal-usager work show that the stack keeps expanding around the core DUI.
- AI is framed as a synthesis and drafting assistant, not a replacement for professional judgment.
- RH and planning are positioned as part of the same operational system as the dossier.

## Implication for DMG

- The corpus strengthens the view that IMAGO already covers a mature baseline.
- DMG should stay framed as a non-invasive layer only if it adds a clear decision or governance benefit.
- The strongest opening remains a narrow layer that improves signal, not a replacement stack.
