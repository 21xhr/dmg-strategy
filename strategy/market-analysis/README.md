# Market Analysis

## Purpose

This folder prepares the repo for deeper market and competitor research.

The goal is to separate:

- strategic product positioning
- reusable competitive categories
- deeper case-by-case research on real companies or products

## Recommended structure

- `competitors.md`: high-level map of competitor categories and why they matter
- `competitors/`: one file per serious company or product worth deeper analysis
- future optional files for buyer categories, market signals, or pricing benchmarks if research becomes deeper

Within `competitors/`, it is useful to keep one level of clustering in mind even if it is not represented as subfolders yet.

For example:

- Make.org, Decidim, and Go Vocal currently form a civitech and participation-infrastructure cluster
- their strongest lessons for DMG should be separated into what is environment-specific versus what may generalize across other verticals

## Working rule

Each competitor file should answer:

- why this company or product matters
- what is actually verified versus still to verify
- where the overlap with DMG is real
- where the product model differs
- what the existence of that competitor proves about the market
